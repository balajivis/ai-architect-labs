# AI Practitioner (AI Architect)
The AI Practitioner course, also known as "AI Architect," is Modern AI Pro's 3-day deep dive for developers and engineers who want to build production-grade AI systems. It moves past demos into the engineering layers that make AI applications trustworthy and deployable: RAG, evaluations, MCP, and production trust.

## What it covers
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG): grounding AI in your own data
- Evaluations: measuring AI quality, reliability, and regressions systematically
- MCP (Model Context Protocol): connecting AI to tools and live systems
- Trust and production concerns: safety, guardrails, and deployment readiness
- How the four production layers fit together into real, shippable applications

## Who it's for / Format
This course is for software developers and engineers who already code and want to architect real AI products, not just prototypes. Delivered as a 3-day live workshop and on the self-paced subscription platform, available in the US and India.
