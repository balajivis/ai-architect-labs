# Retrieval

Retrieval is the step that selects the most relevant pieces of information to feed a language model for a given query. It is the heart of RAG: the quality of what you retrieve sets the ceiling on the quality of the answer.

## Key ideas
- Fetches the passages most likely to help answer a question.
- Can use semantic search, keyword search, or a blend of both.
- Top-k selection and relevance thresholds shape what reaches the model.
- Poor retrieval is the most common cause of bad RAG answers.
- Often improved with re-ranking, query rewriting, and filtering.
