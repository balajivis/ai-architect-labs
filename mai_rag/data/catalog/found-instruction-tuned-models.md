# Instruction-tuned Models

Instruction-tuned models are base models further adapted to follow natural-language instructions and behave helpfully in a conversational setting. This is what makes a model usable as an assistant that responds to requests rather than just continuing text.

## Key ideas
- They are trained to follow instructions, not merely predict the next words.
- This step turns a raw base model into a usable assistant.
- It improves helpfulness, formatting, and task-following.
- It is distinct from later alignment steps that shape tone and safety.
- Most chat-style models you interact with are instruction-tuned.
