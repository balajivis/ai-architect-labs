# Stakeholder Alignment for AI

AI initiatives touch many groups — leadership, engineering, legal, security, and end users — often with different expectations and concerns. Stakeholder alignment is the work of building shared understanding of goals, risks, and trade-offs so a project can move forward.

## Key ideas
- AI projects raise distinct concerns: accuracy, risk, cost, and ethics.
- Alignment means surfacing assumptions and agreeing on goals and guardrails early.
- Different audiences need different framing of the same decision.
- Early agreement on success metrics prevents conflict later.
