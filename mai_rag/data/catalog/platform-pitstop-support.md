# Pitstop 24/7 Support
Pitstop is Modern AI Pro's always-on support system, built on the promise that you're "never stuck for more than an hour." It gives every learner round-the-clock help so momentum never stalls, whether you hit a confusing concept at midnight or a broken step on a weekend.

## What it is
- 24/7 learner support designed to unblock you fast
- The core promise: never stuck for more than an hour
- Help spanning concepts, tools, and course exercises
- A safety net that keeps learners moving through the L1–L6 journey
- Complements human Office Hours and Community channels

## Who it's for / Format
Pitstop serves every Modern AI Pro learner across all courses and segments, from career switchers to engineers. It is part of the self-paced subscription platform experience and supports learners in both the US and India.
