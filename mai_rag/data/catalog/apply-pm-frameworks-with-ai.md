# PM Frameworks with AI

Product managers use classic frameworks — prioritization, opportunity sizing, problem framing — to decide what to build. AI assistants can accelerate these by drafting first passes, surfacing blind spots, and pressure-testing assumptions. The goal is faster, better-reasoned decisions, not replacing judgment.

## Key ideas
- Familiar frameworks (RICE, MoSCoW, jobs-to-be-done, North Star metrics) can be scaffolded with AI prompts.
- AI is useful for divergent thinking: generating options, counter-arguments, and edge cases a team might miss.
- Human judgment still owns the final call; AI output is a draft to critique, not a decision.
- Structured inputs (clear goals, constraints, user context) produce far more useful AI assistance.
