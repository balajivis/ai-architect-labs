# AI Career Coaching at Modern AI Pro
AI career coaching is central to how Modern AI Pro keeps learning relevant to real outcomes. Rather than handing everyone the same syllabus, the platform personalizes the journey around each learner's role, background, and goals.

## What it is
- A personalized journey adapted to each learner's career goals
- Recommendations tuned to background, pace, and target outcomes
- Guidance through the L1–L6 ladder so learners always know the next step
- A connection between course content and real career progression
- Especially valuable for career switchers charting a new path

## Who it's for / Format
AI career coaching supports every Modern AI Pro learner, with particular value for mid-career switchers and professionals targeting specific roles. It is part of the self-paced subscription platform, available in the US and India.
