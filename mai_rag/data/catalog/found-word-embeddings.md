# Word Embeddings (Word2Vec)

Word embeddings represent words as points in a numeric space so that similar meanings sit close together. Early approaches like Word2Vec showed that meaning could be captured geometrically, an idea central to modern AI and search.

## Key ideas
- Words become vectors of numbers that capture meaning.
- Similar words end up near each other in the space.
- This enables similarity search and semantic comparison.
- Word2Vec popularized the idea historically.
- Embeddings now extend to sentences, documents, and images.
