# GraphRAG and Knowledge Graphs

GraphRAG combines retrieval with a knowledge graph — a structured network of entities and their relationships — so the system can answer questions that span many documents. Knowledge graphs add explicit connections that flat text chunks lack.

## Key ideas
- Represents entities and relationships as a navigable graph.
- Supports questions that require connecting facts across sources.
- Enables summarization over communities or clusters of related nodes.
- Complements vector retrieval rather than replacing it.
- Adds upfront effort to build and maintain the graph.
