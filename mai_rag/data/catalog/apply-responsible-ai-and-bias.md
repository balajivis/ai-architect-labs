# Responsible AI and Bias

Responsible AI is about building systems that are fair, transparent, and accountable. Because models learn from data, they can absorb and amplify bias — so teams need to actively check for unfair or harmful outcomes across different groups of users.

## Key ideas
- Bias can enter through training data, design choices, or deployment context.
- Responsible AI emphasizes fairness, transparency, privacy, and accountability.
- Detecting bias requires testing outcomes across user groups, not just averages.
- Mitigation is ongoing — monitored after launch, not solved once.
