# Agentic AI for Leaders
Agentic AI for Leaders is a 2-day course designed for business leaders and executives who need to make confident strategic decisions about AI. It focuses on understanding agentic AI — autonomous, tool-using systems — and what they mean for strategy, teams, and competitive advantage, without requiring any coding.

## What it covers
- What agentic AI is and how it differs from simple chatbots and assistants
- Where AI agents create real business value and where they fall short
- Strategic frameworks for evaluating, prioritizing, and adopting AI initiatives
- Risk, governance, and trust considerations leaders must own
- Leading AI-enabled teams and building an AI-forward organization

## Who it's for / Format
This course is for non-technical business leaders, executives, and decision-makers responsible for AI strategy and adoption. Delivered as a 2-day live workshop and on the self-paced subscription platform, available in the US and India.
