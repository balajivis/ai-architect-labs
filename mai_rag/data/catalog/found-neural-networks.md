# Neural Networks

Neural networks are the foundational machine learning structures inspired loosely by how neurons connect. They learn patterns from data by adjusting many simple connections, and they underpin modern AI including language models.

## Key ideas
- They learn patterns from examples rather than explicit rules.
- They are built from many simple, connected units.
- Layers let them capture increasingly abstract features.
- They power vision, speech, and language systems.
- Transformers are a specialized kind of neural network.
