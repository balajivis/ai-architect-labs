# CrewAI and AutoGen Overview

CrewAI and AutoGen are popular frameworks for building multi-agent systems. They make it easier to define several agents, give them roles, and have them collaborate on a task — each with its own style and conventions.

## Key ideas
- Both frameworks specialize in teams of cooperating agents.
- CrewAI emphasizes role-based "crews" working toward a goal.
- AutoGen emphasizes conversational collaboration between agents.
- Each provides patterns for delegation, handoffs, and coordination.
- The choice depends on how you want agents to interact.
