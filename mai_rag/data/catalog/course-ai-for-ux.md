# AI for UX
AI for UX is a 1-day course for UX designers who want to design great experiences with and for AI. It covers both using AI to accelerate design work and the unique patterns of designing interfaces where AI is a core part of the product.

## What it covers
- Using AI tools to speed up research, ideation, and design workflows
- Design patterns for AI-powered and conversational interfaces
- Designing for uncertainty: handling AI errors, trust, and transparency
- Collaborating with PMs and engineers on AI feature design
- Crafting experiences that set the right user expectations of AI

## Who it's for / Format
This course is for UX and product designers working on AI-enabled products or wanting to adopt AI in their craft. Delivered as a 1-day live workshop and on the self-paced subscription platform, available in the US and India.
