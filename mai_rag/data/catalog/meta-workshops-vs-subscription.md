# Workshops vs the Subscription Platform
Modern AI Pro is delivered in two complementary formats: live workshops and a self-paced subscription platform. Many learners use both — a workshop for intensive, guided learning and the platform for ongoing practice and reference.

## What it is
- Live workshops: instructor-led, time-boxed sessions (1 to 3 days by course)
- Self-paced platform: learn on your own schedule via a subscription
- Shared content: Explainers, Library, Playground, Capstones across both
- Continuous support: 24/7 Pitstop plus Community & Office Hours
- A path that lets learners go intensive, self-paced, or both

## Who it's for / Format
This helps prospective learners choose how to learn with Modern AI Pro based on schedule and learning style. Both formats are available in the US and India.
