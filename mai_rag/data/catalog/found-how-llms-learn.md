# How LLMs Learn

Large language models learn by processing huge amounts of text and adjusting internal parameters to better predict what comes next. Over many examples, this simple objective produces surprisingly broad language ability.

## Key ideas
- Learning is driven by predicting the next piece of text.
- Patterns emerge from scale, not hand-written rules.
- Internal parameters are adjusted gradually over training.
- Capability comes from data breadth plus model size.
- Later tuning steps shape behavior beyond raw prediction.
