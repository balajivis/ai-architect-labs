# AI Roadmapping

A roadmap sequences what a team will build and when. Roadmapping AI features adds uncertainty — model capabilities shift, quality is probabilistic, and feasibility is harder to predict. Good AI roadmaps stay outcome-focused and leave room to learn.

## Key ideas
- AI roadmaps should anchor on outcomes and problems, not specific models.
- Uncertainty is higher; plan for experiments and validation gates.
- Quick prototypes de-risk feasibility before committing to delivery dates.
- Roadmaps must account for evals, guardrails, and ongoing monitoring, not just launch.
