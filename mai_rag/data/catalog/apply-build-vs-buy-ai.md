# Build vs Buy AI

Teams adopting AI face a recurring choice: build a capability in-house, or buy/adopt an existing model or vendor. The decision weighs cost, control, speed, differentiation, and risk — and there's rarely one right answer for every feature.

## Key ideas
- "Buy" (APIs, vendors) is faster to start; "build" offers more control and differentiation.
- Consider total cost, data sensitivity, vendor lock-in, and maintenance burden.
- Differentiating capabilities may justify building; commodity ones rarely do.
- Hybrid approaches are common — buy the model, build the product layer around it.
