# AI for PMs
AI for PMs is a focused 1-day course for product managers who need to ship AI-powered products and work effectively with AI-enabled teams. It equips PMs to scope, prioritize, and reason about AI features with realistic expectations of what the technology can and cannot do.

## What it covers
- How to identify and scope high-value AI product opportunities
- Understanding AI capabilities and limitations well enough to write good specs
- Collaborating with engineers on RAG, evaluations, and agentic features
- Defining quality, success metrics, and guardrails for AI products
- Managing risk, trust, and user expectations in AI-driven experiences

## Who it's for / Format
This course is for product managers and product leaders building or planning AI features. Delivered as a 1-day live workshop and on the self-paced subscription platform, available in the US and India.
