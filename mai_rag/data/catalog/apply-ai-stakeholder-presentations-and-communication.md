(Merged: "AI Stakeholder Presentations" + "AI Communication Design" — overlapping topics combined.) Communicating product decisions clearly is core PM work. ## Key ideas
- AI can draft presentation outlines, executive summaries, and audience-specific framing.
- Good communication design means matching depth and tone to the audience.
- AI helps tighten wording and surface a clearer story, but the argument must be the PM's own.
- Visual and verbal clarity reduce misalignment and speed up decisions.
