# AI Governance

AI governance is the set of policies, roles, and processes that ensure AI systems are used responsibly and in line with legal and ethical standards. As AI moves into products, organizations need clear accountability for how models are built, deployed, and monitored.

## Key ideas
- Governance defines who is accountable for AI decisions and outcomes.
- Policies cover data use, model approval, monitoring, and incident response.
- Emerging regulations (such as the EU AI Act) push risk-based oversight.
- Governance balances innovation speed with safety, fairness, and compliance.
