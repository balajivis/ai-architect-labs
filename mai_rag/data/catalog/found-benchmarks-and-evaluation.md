# Benchmarks and Evaluation

Benchmarks and evaluation are how we measure whether a model or AI system actually performs well. Public benchmarks give rough comparisons, while task-specific evaluation tells you whether a system works for your real use case.

## Key ideas
- Benchmarks offer broad, comparable signals of capability.
- They can be gamed or fail to match real-world tasks.
- Custom evaluation on your own data is more trustworthy.
- Evaluation should cover quality, safety, and reliability.
- "Looks good in a demo" is not the same as evaluated.
