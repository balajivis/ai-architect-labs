# Which Course Should I Take?
Modern AI Pro's catalog is organized by career function, so the right course depends on your role and goals. Most learners start with AI Essentials for a shared foundation, then specialize.

## What it covers
- New to AI or non-technical: start with AI Essentials (3 days)
- Developers/engineers building AI products: AI Practitioner / AI Architect (3 days), then Multi-Agent Systems or MCP Engineering
- Developers wanting faster coding: Vibe Coding / AI for Developers (2 days)
- Business leaders and executives: Agentic AI for Leaders (2 days)
- Product managers: AI for PMs (1 day); UX designers: AI for UX (1 day)

## Who it's for / Format
This guide helps any prospective learner map their role to the right Modern AI Pro course. All courses are offered as live workshops and on the self-paced subscription platform, in the US and India.
