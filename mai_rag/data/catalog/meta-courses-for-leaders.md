# Courses for Business Leaders
Non-technical leaders and executives have a focused path through Modern AI Pro centered on AI strategy and confident decision-making — no coding required. The catalog lets leaders go as deep as their role demands.

## What it covers
- Agentic AI for Leaders (2 days): the core strategy course for executives
- AI Essentials (3 days): recommended foundation for a shared vocabulary
- Strategic frameworks for evaluating and prioritizing AI initiatives
- Risk, governance, and trust considerations leaders must own
- Guidance on leading AI-enabled teams and organizations

## Who it's for / Format
This guide is for executives, managers, and decision-makers responsible for AI strategy. Courses are delivered as live workshops and on the self-paced subscription platform, available in the US and India.
