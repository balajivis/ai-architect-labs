# Enterprise Guardrails and Safety

Guardrails are the controls that keep AI systems within safe, acceptable bounds — filtering harmful content, blocking sensitive data leaks, and catching off-policy behavior. In enterprise settings, guardrails and human oversight protect users, brand, and compliance.

## Key ideas
- Guardrails constrain inputs and outputs to reduce harmful or off-policy responses.
- Safety layers include content moderation, sensitive-data handling, and escalation paths.
- Human-in-the-loop review adds a checkpoint for high-stakes actions.
- Effective guardrails use AI/ML classifiers and policies, not brittle keyword rules.
