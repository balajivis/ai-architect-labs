The L1–L6 learning ladder is Modern AI Pro's structured progression that takes learners from first principles to advanced, applied capability. It organizes the journey into six levels so progress is clear, sequenced, and never overwhelming.

## What it is

- A clear map of where a learner is and what comes next
- A backbone that ties courses, Explainers, and Capstones together
- A framework the AI Career Coach uses to personalize paths
- A way to make a big journey feel achievable, level by level
