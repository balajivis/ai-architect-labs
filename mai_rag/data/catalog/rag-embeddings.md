# Embeddings

Embeddings turn text (or images, audio, etc.) into numeric vectors that capture meaning, so that similar content lands close together in vector space. They are the foundation that makes semantic search and RAG possible.

## Key ideas
- Convert content into dense vectors that encode semantic meaning.
- Similar meanings produce vectors that are close; unrelated ones are far apart.
- Produced by embedding models, which vary in quality, size, and language coverage.
- The same model should be used for both indexing and querying.
- Choice of model affects retrieval quality, cost, and dimension size.
