# Multi-Agent Systems
Multi-Agent Systems is an advanced Modern AI Pro course for engineers building applications where several AI agents collaborate to solve complex tasks. It explores how to design, coordinate, and orchestrate agents that plan, delegate, and work together reliably.

## What it covers
- Core concepts of multi-agent architectures and orchestration
- Agent roles, coordination patterns, and task delegation
- Managing shared context, memory, and communication between agents
- Reliability, oversight, and trust in multi-agent workflows
- Where multi-agent designs outperform single-agent approaches — and where they don't

## Who it's for / Format
This course is for developers and engineers who have foundational AI-building skills and want to architect sophisticated agentic systems. Offered through Modern AI Pro's live workshops and self-paced subscription platform, available in the US and India.
