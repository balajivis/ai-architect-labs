# Modern AI Pro Platform Overview
Modern AI Pro is a learning platform that helps mid-career professionals and teams build real AI skills, with courses aligned to career function and a rich self-paced experience. It pairs intensive live workshops with an always-on subscription platform.

## What it is
- A catalog of career-aligned courses from AI Essentials to advanced engineering tracks
- Interactive Explainers, the Playground, and Capstone Projects for hands-on learning
- The Library: modules, glossary, mindmaps, tools directory, and cheat sheets
- 24/7 Pitstop support, Community & Office Hours, and AI Pulse news
- A personalizing AI Career Coach and an L1–L6 learning ladder

## Who it's for / Format
Modern AI Pro serves career switchers, engineers, leaders, PMs, and UX designers. It is delivered as live workshops and a self-paced subscription platform, available in the US and India.
