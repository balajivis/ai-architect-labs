# The Four Production Layers — RAG, Evals, MCP, Trust
Modern AI Pro teaches that the gap between an AI demo and a production system comes down to four layers most tutorials skip. The AI Practitioner / AI Architect course is built around mastering them.

## What it covers
- RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation): grounding AI in your own data
- Evaluations: systematically measuring quality, reliability, and regressions
- MCP (Model Context Protocol): connecting AI to tools and live systems
- Trust: safety, guardrails, and production readiness
- How the four layers combine into deployable, dependable applications

## Who it's for / Format
This is for developers and engineers who want to build real AI products, not prototypes — covered in depth in the AI Practitioner course, with MCP Engineering and Multi-Agent Systems going further. Live workshops and self-paced platform, in the US and India.
