# AI Essentials
AI Essentials is Modern AI Pro's 3-day foundation course built for everyone, regardless of technical background. It establishes the shared vocabulary, mental models, and hands-on comfort that every other course in the catalog assumes, making it the recommended starting point for the full learning journey.

## What it covers
- Core concepts behind modern AI: how large language models work at an intuitive level
- Practical prompting and how to get reliable results from AI tools
- The landscape of AI capabilities, limitations, and where they fit real work
- A first hands-on tour of using AI assistants safely and effectively
- Orientation to the Modern AI Pro platform, the Library, and the L1–L6 ladder

## Who it's for / Format
AI Essentials is for everyone — career switchers, leaders, PMs, designers, and engineers who want a common foundation before specializing. Delivered as a 3-day live workshop and on the self-paced subscription platform, available in both the US and India.
