# AI-Assisted PRDs

A product requirements document captures the problem, users, goals, and scope of what a team will build. AI tools can help draft, structure, and refine PRDs — turning rough notes into a clear first version a team can react to. This shortens the blank-page phase while keeping authorship with the PM.

## Key ideas
- AI can convert scattered inputs (notes, transcripts, tickets) into a structured PRD draft.
- Good PRDs still need a human-defined problem statement, success metrics, and scope boundaries.
- AI is helpful for spotting gaps: missing edge cases, unclear acceptance criteria, vague goals.
- Iterating with AI ("what's ambiguous here?") often improves clarity faster than solo editing.
