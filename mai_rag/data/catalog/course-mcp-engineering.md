# MCP Engineering
MCP Engineering is a specialized Modern AI Pro course on the Model Context Protocol (MCP), the open standard for connecting AI models to tools, data, and live systems. It teaches engineers how to design and build robust MCP integrations that let AI act reliably in the real world.

## What it covers
- What MCP is and why it matters for production AI applications
- Building MCP servers that expose tools, resources, and data to AI
- Connecting AI agents to external systems safely and reliably
- Patterns for trustworthy, observable tool use
- Fitting MCP into the broader production stack alongside RAG and evals

## Who it's for / Format
This course is for developers and engineers who want to give AI systems dependable access to tools and data through the MCP standard. Offered through Modern AI Pro's live workshops and self-paced subscription platform, available in the US and India.
