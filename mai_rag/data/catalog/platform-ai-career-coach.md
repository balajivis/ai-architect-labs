# AI Career Coach
The AI Career Coach is Modern AI Pro's personalization engine that tailors each learner's journey to their goals and background. Rather than a one-size-fits-all path, it adapts recommendations so learners progress in the direction that fits their career.

## What it is
- Personalizes the learning journey to each learner's goals
- Adapts course and module recommendations to background and pace
- Helps learners navigate the L1–L6 ladder with direction
- Connects learning to real career outcomes
- A guide that keeps the journey relevant and motivating

## Who it's for / Format
The AI Career Coach is for every Modern AI Pro learner, especially career switchers and professionals targeting specific outcomes. It is part of the self-paced subscription platform, available in the US and India.
