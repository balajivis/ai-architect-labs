# Prompt Engineering for PMs

Prompt engineering is the practice of writing clear instructions that get reliable, useful results from AI models. For product managers, it's a practical skill for drafting docs, analyzing feedback, and prototyping AI features without writing code.

## Key ideas
- Clear context, explicit goals, and examples improve the quality of AI responses.
- Iterating on prompts — refining and constraining — is normal and expected.
- Specifying format and audience makes outputs more directly usable.
- Prompts are a product surface too: they shape how AI features behave for users.
