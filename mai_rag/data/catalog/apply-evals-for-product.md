# Evals for Product

Evals are structured ways to measure whether an AI feature is actually doing its job — accuracy, helpfulness, safety, and consistency. For product teams, evals turn "it feels better" into evidence, and catch regressions before users do.

## Key ideas
- Evals measure AI quality against defined criteria, not just vibes.
- Common dimensions include correctness, relevance, tone, and safety.
- Approaches range from human review to automated and model-graded scoring.
- A good eval set evolves as the product and edge cases grow.
