# Transformers Architecture

The transformer is the neural network design behind most modern language models. Its key innovation, attention, lets the model weigh the relationships between words in a sequence, which made today's large models practical.

## Key ideas
- Transformers underpin most current LLMs.
- Attention lets the model relate different parts of the input.
- The design scales well to large data and large models.
- It largely replaced earlier sequence architectures.
- Understanding it helps demystify why LLMs behave as they do.
