# Community & Office Hours
Community & Office Hours is Modern AI Pro's human layer — peer connection plus regular live time with instructors. It ensures learners aren't going it alone, pairing a supportive community with scheduled sessions to get questions answered live.

## What it is
- A learner community for peer support and discussion
- Regular Office Hours with instructors for live help
- Human guidance that complements 24/7 Pitstop support
- A place to share progress, ask questions, and stay accountable
- Connection across segments and courses

## Who it's for / Format
Community & Office Hours serve every Modern AI Pro learner who values peer connection and live instructor access. They are part of the self-paced subscription platform experience and live workshops, available in the US and India.
