# AI Competitive Analysis

Competitive analysis maps how rival products position, price, and differentiate. AI tools can speed up gathering and summarizing public information, then help organize it into comparisons a team can act on. The PM still validates accuracy and draws the strategic conclusions.

## Key ideas
- AI can summarize public materials (sites, reviews, docs) into structured competitor profiles.
- Comparison matrices — features, pricing tiers, positioning — are faster to draft with AI.
- AI output must be fact-checked; models can hallucinate features or stale details.
- The value is in synthesis and "so what?" framing, which remains a human strategic task.
