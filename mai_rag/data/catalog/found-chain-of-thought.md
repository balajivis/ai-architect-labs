# Chain of Thought

Chain of thought is a prompting approach that encourages a model to work through a problem in steps before giving a final answer. It often improves accuracy on reasoning, math, and multi-step tasks.

## Key ideas
- It prompts step-by-step reasoning over instant answers.
- It tends to help on complex, multi-step problems.
- It can add length, latency, and cost.
- It is less useful for simple lookups.
- It pairs naturally with reasoning-oriented models.
