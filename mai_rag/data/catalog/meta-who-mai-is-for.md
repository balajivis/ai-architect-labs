# Who Modern AI Pro Is For
Modern AI Pro is built for a broad range of professionals, with courses aligned to specific career functions and segments. Whether you write code or never will, there's a tailored path.

## What it is
- Career switchers: non-coders, often mid-career, retraining for AI roles
- Elite engineers: experienced developers building production AI systems
- Non-technical leaders: executives setting AI strategy
- Product managers: shipping AI features and leading AI-enabled teams
- UX designers: designing for and with AI

## Who it's for / Format
This overview helps anyone determine whether Modern AI Pro fits their background and goals. Courses span 1 to 3 days as live workshops and are available on the self-paced subscription platform, in the US and India.
