# AI Adoption and Change Management

Introducing AI into an organization is as much about people as technology. Adoption and change management cover how teams learn new tools, adjust workflows, and build trust — so that AI investments actually deliver value rather than gathering dust.

## Key ideas
- Adoption depends on trust, training, and clear value, not just access to tools.
- Change management addresses workflow shifts, roles, and concerns about AI.
- Early wins and champions help spread adoption across teams.
- Measuring real usage and outcomes shows whether AI is delivering value.
