# Machine Learning Algorithms

Machine learning algorithms are the classic methods that learn patterns from data, including approaches for classification, regression, and clustering. They remain highly effective for structured, tabular problems where LLMs are not the right fit.

## Key ideas
- They cover tasks like prediction, grouping, and classification.
- Many work best on structured, tabular data.
- They are often cheaper and more interpretable than LLMs.
- They underpin much of pre-LLM AI in industry.
- Choosing classic ML vs an LLM is a practical decision.
