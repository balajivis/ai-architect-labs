# Guardrails

Guardrails are the safety and quality checks placed around a model's inputs and outputs — blocking unsafe requests, filtering sensitive content, and keeping responses on-policy. In RAG systems they protect against injected content and off-limits answers.

## Key ideas
- Screen inputs and outputs for safety, policy, and quality.
- Catch prompt injection, sensitive data, and out-of-scope requests.
- Implemented with model/ML-based classifiers, not brittle keyword rules.
- Layered before, during, and after generation.
- Balance protection against over-blocking legitimate use.
