# Capstone Projects
Capstone Projects are Modern AI Pro's culminating, real-world builds where learners apply everything they've learned to a substantial project. They turn knowledge into demonstrable, portfolio-worthy work — for example, building a stock-picking agent.

## What it is
- End-to-end projects that integrate skills across a course
- Real, tangible builds such as a stock-picking agent
- A way to prove and showcase applied AI ability
- The practical payoff of the L1–L6 learning journey
- Portfolio pieces that demonstrate readiness for real work

## Who it's for / Format
Capstone Projects are for learners ready to consolidate their skills into a finished build, across segments from career switchers to engineers. They are part of the self-paced subscription platform and reinforced in live workshops, available in the US and India.
