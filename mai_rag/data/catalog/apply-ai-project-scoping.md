# AI Project Scoping

Scoping an AI project means defining the problem, success criteria, data needs, and boundaries before building. Because AI quality is probabilistic and data-dependent, tight scoping is what keeps projects from sprawling or quietly failing.

## Key ideas
- Start from a clear problem and measurable definition of "good enough."
- Assess data availability and quality early — they often decide feasibility.
- Define what's out of scope to avoid scope creep and unrealistic promises.
- Plan for evaluation and monitoring as part of the scope, not an afterthought.
