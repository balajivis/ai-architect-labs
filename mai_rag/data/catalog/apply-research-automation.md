# Research Automation

Product and UX research involves collecting, summarizing, and synthesizing user feedback, interviews, and market signals. AI can automate repetitive parts — transcribing, clustering themes, drafting summaries — so teams spend more time on insight and less on busywork.

## Key ideas
- AI can transcribe interviews and group feedback into recurring themes for review.
- Summaries and affinity-style clustering are good first drafts, not final findings.
- Automation reduces grunt work but risks losing nuance; humans confirm the themes are real.
- Privacy and consent matter when feeding user data into AI tools.

## At Modern AI Pro
The AI for PMs and AI for UX courses introduce research-automation workflows at an overview level, stressing that AI accelerates synthesis without replacing the researcher's judgment.
