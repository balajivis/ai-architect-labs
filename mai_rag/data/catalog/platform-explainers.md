# Interactive Explainers
Explainers are Modern AI Pro's interactive, animated walkthroughs that make hard AI concepts click. Instead of dense text, they use motion and visuals to show how ideas work, forming a major part of the platform's learning experience.

## What it is
- Interactive, animated visual explanations of AI concepts
- Designed to turn abstract ideas into intuitive understanding
- A core teaching surface across the platform, not a side feature
- Visual storytelling that complements hands-on practice
- Built to serve learners across all backgrounds and segments

## Who it's for / Format
Explainers are for every Modern AI Pro learner, especially those who learn best visually. They are part of the self-paced subscription platform, available to learners in the US and India.
