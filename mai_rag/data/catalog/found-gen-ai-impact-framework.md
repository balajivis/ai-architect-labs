# Gen AI Impact Framework

A Gen AI impact framework is a structured way to decide where generative AI actually adds value in a business or workflow. It helps teams prioritize high-value, feasible use cases instead of adopting AI for its own sake.

## Key ideas
- It weighs value against feasibility and risk.
- It steers effort toward high-impact use cases.
- It guards against "AI everywhere" hype.
- It considers cost, reliability, and human oversight.
- It turns AI adoption into a deliberate decision.
