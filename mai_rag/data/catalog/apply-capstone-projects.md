# Capstone Projects

A capstone is a culminating project where learners apply everything they've studied to a realistic, end-to-end problem. In AI education, capstones turn concepts into demonstrable work — a portfolio piece that shows real capability to employers and peers.

## Key ideas
- Capstones integrate multiple skills into one substantial deliverable.
- They emphasize end-to-end ownership: problem, build, evaluation, and presentation.
- Realistic constraints make the experience closer to actual work.
- The result doubles as a portfolio artifact demonstrating applied ability.

## At Modern AI Pro
Modern AI Pro courses culminate in capstone projects at an overview level, where learners apply their skills to a realistic AI problem end to end.
