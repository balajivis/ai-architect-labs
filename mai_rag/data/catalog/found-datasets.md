# Datasets

Datasets are the collections of examples used to train and evaluate AI models. Their size, quality, and representativeness strongly shape how a model behaves, including its strengths, gaps, and biases.

## Key ideas
- Data quality often matters more than data quantity.
- Bias and gaps in data carry into model behavior.
- Separate data is needed for training and for evaluation.
- Licensing and provenance of data matter increasingly.
- Curating data is a core, underrated part of AI work.
