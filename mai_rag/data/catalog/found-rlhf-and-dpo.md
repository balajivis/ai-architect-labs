# RLHF and DPO

RLHF (reinforcement learning from human feedback) and DPO (direct preference optimization) are alignment techniques that use human preferences to make a model more helpful, honest, and safe. They shape how a model responds beyond raw prediction.

## Key ideas
- They align model behavior with human preferences.
- They improve helpfulness, tone, and safety.
- They come after pre-training and instruction tuning.
- DPO is a more direct alternative to preference-based RL.
- Alignment is about behavior, not just raw capability.
