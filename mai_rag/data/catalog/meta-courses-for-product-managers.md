# Courses for Product Managers
Product managers have a clear path through Modern AI Pro built around shipping AI products and working with AI-enabled teams. The catalog lets PMs go as deep as their role requires.

## What it covers
- AI for PMs (1 day): the core course for scoping and shipping AI features
- AI Essentials (3 days): recommended foundation if you're newer to AI
- Agentic AI for Leaders (2 days): useful for PMs with strategic/leadership scope
- AI for UX (1 day): valuable for PMs partnering closely with designers
- A path from fundamentals to confident collaboration with engineers

## Who it's for / Format
This guide is for product managers and product leaders choosing among Modern AI Pro courses. All are delivered as live workshops and on the self-paced subscription platform, available in the US and India.
