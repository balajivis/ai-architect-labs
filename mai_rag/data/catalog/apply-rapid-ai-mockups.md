# Rapid AI Mockups

AI tools can turn a written description into wireframes, mockups, or clickable prototypes in minutes. This lets teams visualize ideas early, get feedback sooner, and iterate before committing engineering time. Mockups are for communication and learning, not production code.

## Key ideas
- Text-to-mockup and AI design tools speed up the idea-to-visual loop.
- Rapid prototypes are best for testing concepts and aligning stakeholders, not final UI.
- Iterating on a visual draft surfaces usability questions earlier than specs alone.
- Generated designs still need a human eye for accessibility, brand, and feasibility.
