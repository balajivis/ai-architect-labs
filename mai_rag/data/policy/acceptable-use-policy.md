# Acceptable Use Policy

## 1. Scope

This policy governs use of Northwind Technologies' computer systems, networks, software, data, and communications infrastructure. This includes company-owned and personal devices used to access company systems.

## 2. Permitted Uses

Company systems are provided for business purposes. Incidental personal use is permitted provided it:
- Does not interfere with work responsibilities
- Does not consume significant company resources
- Does not violate any local law
- Is not used for commercial gain outside Northwind

## 3. Prohibited Uses

The following activities are strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action, termination, and/or legal action:

### 3.1 Illegal Activity
- Hacking, unauthorized access, or attempts to circumvent security controls
- Copyright infringement, piracy, or unauthorized distribution of software
- Fraud, extortion, or blackmail
- Possession or distribution of child exploitation material
- Any activity that violates local, state, or federal law

### 3.2 Harassment and Discrimination
- Sending threatening, defamatory, or sexually explicit messages
- Bullying or harassment of colleagues
- Discrimination based on race, gender, age, religion, sexual orientation, or disability
- Posting demeaning comments about colleagues or competitors

### 3.3 Confidentiality Violations
- Sharing passwords, access credentials, or authentication tokens
- Forwarding confidential business information to personal email
- Discussing customer names, contracts, or product roadmaps outside of authorized channels
- Recording video or audio of company meetings without explicit permission

### 3.4 Malware and Spam
- Installing unauthorized software or browser extensions
- Attempting to disable antivirus or endpoint security software
- Sending spam or unsolicited mass emails
- Distributing viruses, trojans, or other malicious code
- Accessing the corporate network with a personal device that is not enrolled in MDM

### 3.5 Resource Abuse
- Mining cryptocurrency on company systems
- Using company internet for streaming video without approval (except for short breaks)
- Downloading or storing large personal media files on company storage
- Running unauthorized servers or services on company network

## 4. Monitoring and Enforcement

Northwind reserves the right to monitor all network traffic, email, and file storage. Employees have no expectation of privacy when using company systems. Logs are retained for 90 days and may be reviewed during investigations.

Users who violate this policy may face:
- Suspension or revocation of system access
- Mandatory security training
- Written warning
- Suspension without pay
- Termination of employment

## 5. Personal Responsibility

All users are responsible for:
- Protecting their login credentials and not sharing them
- Locking their workstation when away from desk (minimum 5 minutes)
- Not leaving printed documents with sensitive information unattended
- Reporting suspected unauthorized access immediately to IT

## 6. Exceptions

Authorized researchers and security professionals may conduct penetration testing or security research only with written approval from the VP Security. The **Information Security Policy** defines required controls for such activities.
