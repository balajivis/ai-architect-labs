# Anti-Harassment & Non-Discrimination Policy

## 1. Policy Statement

Northwind Technologies is committed to providing a work environment free from harassment and discrimination. We prohibit discrimination or harassment based on protected characteristics including race, color, religion, gender, gender expression, age, national origin, disability, genetic information, sexual orientation, or veteran status.

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, volunteers, and third parties conducting business on Northwind premises or via Northwind systems.

## 2. Prohibited Conduct

**2.1 Harassment** is unwelcome conduct, comments, or actions that create an intimidating, hostile, or offensive work environment. Examples include:
- Slurs, jokes, or stereotyping based on protected characteristics
- Unwanted physical contact or invasion of personal space
- Repeated unwelcome advances or requests for romantic/sexual interaction
- Display of offensive materials (images, symbols)
- Threats or intimidation

**2.2 Discrimination** is unfavorable treatment in hiring, compensation, promotion, or other employment decisions based on protected characteristics. This includes:
- Excluding candidates from recruitment based on gender, race, or age
- Denying raises or advancement opportunities based on disability status
- Terminating employment because of religious observance
- Retaliatory actions against employees who report violations

**2.3 Quid pro quo** harassment—conditioning employment benefits (raises, promotions, schedules) on unwelcome sexual conduct or submission to harassment—is strictly prohibited.

**2.4 Retaliation** against anyone who reports harassment, participates in an investigation, or provides testimony is prohibited and will result in discipline.

## 3. Reporting Mechanisms

Employees who experience or witness harassment or discrimination should report immediately:

| Channel | Contact | Confidentiality |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| **Direct Manager** | Manager name on org chart | Low (escalated) |
| **People Operations** | people@northwind.tech | Medium |
| **General Counsel** | legal@northwind.tech | Medium-High |
| **Ethics Hotline** | +1-888-XXX-XXXX (third-party) | High (anonymous option) |

## 4. Investigation Process

Upon receiving a report, People Operations will:

1. **Acknowledge** receipt within 24 hours.
2. **Interview** the complainant, respondent, and witnesses within 5 business days.
3. **Document** findings in a confidential report.
4. **Notify** complainant and respondent of outcome within 15 business days.
5. **Take corrective action** if harassment is substantiated (warning, suspension, termination).

See the Grievance Procedure for the full formal complaint timeline and appeal rights.

## 5. Prevention & Training

All employees must complete anti-harassment training during onboarding (part of the mandatory Day-1 security training block) and annually thereafter. Managers receive additional training on recognizing, preventing, and responding to harassment.

## 6. Non-Retaliation

Employees who report harassment in good faith are protected from retaliation. Any adverse action taken against a reporter (demotion, negative evaluation, schedule change) in response to a report will be treated as a separate violation.

## 7. Accommodations

Employees with disabilities are entitled to reasonable accommodations to participate in work and investigations. Contact People Operations to discuss needs.
