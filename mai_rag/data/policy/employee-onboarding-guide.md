# Employee Onboarding Guide

## 1. Welcome to Northwind Technologies

This guide walks new employees through their first week and first month at Northwind. Your manager will be your primary point of contact; the HR team and IT will handle administrative setup.

**Key contact:** hronboarding@northwind.com or Slack #hr-newemployees

## 2. Before Your Start Date (Day -3 to Day 0)

### 2.1 Pre-Onboarding (HR)
- Background check completed
- Offer letter signed and filed
- I-9 employment verification (US employees)
- Tax forms (W-4, I-9, state forms)
- Emergency contact information collected
- Handbook acknowledgment signed

### 2.2 IT Provisioning (Automatic)
- Okta account created
- Email address activated
- OneDrive / cloud storage provisioned
- VPN profile generated (available on Day 1)
- Hardware order placed: laptop, phone, monitor, peripherals

### 2.3 Manager Preparation
- Office/desk assigned (or home office setup for remote employees)
- GitHub Enterprise access requested for engineering roles
- Cloud environment (AWS/Azure) project access configured
- First-week calendar sent (meet and greets, training sessions)

## 3. First Day (Day 1)

### 3.1 Morning Arrival (In-Office) or Virtual Onboarding (Remote)

**In-office employees:**
- Meet reception staff
- Receive laptop and credentials printout
- IT support assists with VPN, laptop configuration, and WiFi connection
- Receive access card for offices and parking

**Remote employees:**
- Receive laptop and peripherals shipped overnight
- IT support calls to assist with setup (phone number in shipping email)
- Slack invitation sent; meet team in #welcome channel

### 3.2 Mandatory Security and Compliance Training (3 hours)

All employees must complete:

1. **Information Security Awareness** (1 hour)
   - Topics: Data classification, incident reporting, password safety, phishing recognition
   - Assessment: 80% passing required
   
2. **Acceptable Use and Harassment Prevention** (1.5 hours)
   - Topics: Acceptable use of corporate systems, anti-harassment policy, diversity and inclusion
   - Acknowledgment: Sign electronic form
   
3. **Getting Started with Okta and MFA Setup** (0.5 hours)
   - Generate TOTP in authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, or Authy)
   - Test VPN login and connection
   - Password reset walkthrough

### 3.3 First Meeting with Manager
- Review role, responsibilities, and first-month goals
- Discuss team structure, reporting relationships
- Set up recurring one-on-one schedule (typically weekly)
- Assign onboarding buddy (peer mentor from same team)

### 3.4 Lunch with Team (or virtual coffee meeting)
- Meet direct teammates
- Learn about ongoing projects
- Get recommendations for lunch spots (in-office) or local coffee shops (remote)

## 4. First Week (Day 2–5)

### 4.1 System Access and Credentials
- Confirm all cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) are accessible
- Test email and calendar (Outlook)
- Verify GitHub/GitLab/internal repository access
- Confirm VPN connection is stable

### 4.2 Security and Compliance Completions
- Complete data classification and data handling training (see **Data Classification & Retention Policy**)
- Read and acknowledge **Information Security Policy**
- Review **Acceptable Use Policy**
- Review **Identity & Access Management Policy** (pay special attention to MFA and password standards)

### 4.3 Departmental Onboarding

**Engineering employees:**
- Review codebase overview and architecture documentation
- Set up local development environment with manager's help
- Run first build/test to verify setup
- Attend engineering sync meeting

**Sales employees:**
- Learn CRM (Salesforce) and customer database tools
- Review current customer list and pipeline
- Attend sales meeting
- Schedule customer introductions

**Customer Success employees:**
- Review **On-Call & Escalation Policy**
- Learn support ticketing system (Zendesk)
- Shadow existing support agent for calls

**Finance/HR employees:**
- Review financial policies and spending rules
- Access finance dashboard and reporting tools
- Learn procurement process and vendor management

### 4.4 Cultural and Logistics

- Tour office facilities (restrooms, kitchen, emergency exits, parking)
- Review company handbook (benefits, time off, expense policy)
- Add yourself to office Slack channels (#engineering, #sales, #general)
- Attend company culture intro talk (if available during first week)

## 5. First Month (Week 2–4)

### 5.1 Role-Specific Training

Based on your role, you'll complete:
- **Product training**: Hands-on walkthrough of Northwind's product and customer use cases
- **Tools and systems**: Deep dive into internal tools unique to your function
- **Process documentation**: Understand how your team makes decisions, ships changes, and escalates issues

### 5.2 VPN and Remote Access Setup
- If working remotely, ensure VPN is configured and tested daily
- Learn how to identify when to use VPN (see **Remote Access / VPN Guide**)
- Confirm device encryption is enabled (BitLocker/FileVault)

### 5.3 Travel and Expense Policy
- Review **Expense & Travel Policy**: spending limits, approval workflows, receipt requirements
- Add yourself to expense system (Expensify)
- Understand that expenses over $75 require a receipt

### 5.4 Leave and Time-Off (PTO)
- Review **Leave & Time-Off Policy**: accrual, approval process, blackout dates
- Learn how to request time off in Workday
- Understand that PTO accrues at 1.5 days per month
- Coordinate vacation plans with manager

### 5.5 30-Day Check-In with Manager
- Review progress on first-month goals
- Get feedback on performance
- Address any blockers or concerns
- Plan goals for Month 2

## 6. First 90 Days – Probation Period

All new employees have a 90-day probation period during which:
- Performance is evaluated monthly by manager
- You're expected to learn role fundamentals and deliver on defined goals
- Either party can end employment with 1 week notice (versus standard 2-week notice)
- Benefits remain active throughout

Regular check-ins with your manager help ensure you're on track. At 90 days, HR will confirm successful completion of probation.

## 9. Optional – Professional Development

By Month 2, explore:
- Internal training library (LinkedIn Learning, Coursera)
- Monthly lunch-and-learns hosted by different teams
- Conference attendance policy (see **Expense & Travel Policy**)
- Mentorship opportunities with senior staff
