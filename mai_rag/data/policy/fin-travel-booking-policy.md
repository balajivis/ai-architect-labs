# Travel Booking Policy

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This policy establishes procedures for booking business travel at Northwind Technologies, including flights, hotels, rental cars, and ground transportation. All travel must be approved per **Expense & Travel Policy** before booking. This document focuses on *how* to book; approval authority is defined in the main expense policy.

## 2. Travel Approval Workflow (Pre-Booking)

Before booking any travel:

1. **Submit travel request** in Expensify with:
   - Destination and dates
   - Purpose (conference, customer meeting, training, etc.)
   - Estimated total cost (flight + hotel + meals + ground)
   - Traveler name and manager name

2. **Manager approval**: Manager approves within 3 business days
3. **VP approval** (if estimated cost >$2,000): VP approves within 2 business days
4. **Finance approval** (automatic if <$2,500; manual if >$2,500): Finance reviews budget and approves within 1 business day
5. **Book travel**: Only after all approvals are received; do not book before approval

**Best practice**: Submit travel request ≥14 days before travel date to receive lowest fares.

## 3. Flight Booking Guidelines

### 3.1 Booking Class and Carrier

| Flight Duration | Allowed Class | Approval Required |
|---|---|---|
| Domestic, <6 hours | Economy | Manager only |
| Domestic, 6+ hours | Economy (default); Business with VP approval | VP approval for Business |
| International, <8 hours | Economy | VP approval if >$2,000 |
| International, 8+ hours | Economy or Business (VP discretion) | VP approval for Business |

**Preferred carrier**: United Airlines (corporate rate available via Expensify)
**Acceptable carriers**: Any US carrier (Delta, American, Southwest, Alaska, Frontier)
**Booking window**: ≥14 days in advance to ensure lowest fares

### 3.2 Booking Process

1. **Use Expensify app**: Expensify's Flight Finder shows available options and discounts
2. **Compare options**: Filter by price, flight time, layovers
3. **Select option**: Click "Book" in Expensify; auto-completes to your corporate discount
4. **Confirmation email**: Expensify emails itinerary and e-ticket within 1 hour
5. **Add to calendar**: Sync to Outlook/Google Calendar

**Baggage**: Standard baggage (1 checked bag) is covered by company. Extra baggage is employee responsibility.

### 3.3 Special Circumstances

**Connections and layovers**: 
- Prefer nonstop if available (higher productivity)
- Layovers >3 hours in major hub are acceptable if price savings >10%

**Tight connections (<90 min)**:
- Avoid if possible (high risk of missed flight)
- If unavoidable, book at employee's own risk

**Missed flights**:
- If you miss flight due to personal reasons, you are liable for rebooking cost
- If flight is canceled, rebooking is airline responsibility; expense claim includes rebooking fee if incurred

**Standby flights**:
- Not recommended (unpredictable)
- OK only if you have no scheduled return date (flexible return)

## 4. Hotel Booking Guidelines

### 4.1 Hotel Selection and Rates

Northwind has negotiated corporate rates with several hotel chains:
- **Marriott** (all properties): 10–15% discount via corporate code NTW
- **Hilton** (all properties): 12% discount
- **Choice Hotels** (Comfort Inn, Quality Inn, etc.): 10% discount

**Rate limits** (before taxes):

| City Type | Max Nightly Rate |
|---|---|
| Major metro (NYC, SF, LA, Boston) | $250 |
| Mid-size city (Denver, Austin, Atlanta) | $150 |
| Small town | $100 |

All taxes and mandatory resort fees are fully reimbursed (over limit does not apply to taxes/fees).

### 4.2 Hotel Booking Process

1. **Use Expensify's hotel search**: Shows available hotels, corporate rates, and nightly totals
2. **Filter by nightly rate limit**: Expensify auto-filters hotels within your destination's limit
3. **Select hotel**: Book via Expensify; corporate rate is applied
4. **Confirmation email**: Hotel sends itinerary and confirmation number
5. **Check-in**: Present ID and corporate rate confirmation at check-in

**Booking window**: Book ≥7 days in advance to ensure availability and rate lock.

### 4.3 Hotel Incidentals

**Covered**:
- Nightly room charge (up to limit)
- Taxes and mandatory resort fees
- Wi-Fi (if not included)
- Laundry and dry cleaning (trips >5 days)
- Room service and hotel restaurant meals (categorized as meals, subject to per diem limits)

**Not covered**:
- Minibar and alcohol (unless mini-fridge charges for incidentals)
- Pay-per-view movies or entertainment
- Spa, massage, gym (unless hotel fitness facility is free)
- Pet fees (unless bringing company service dog)
- Upgrade to suite or premium room

### 4.4 Hotel Changes or Cancellations

- **Free cancellation**: Most hotel rates allow free cancellation until 11am day of arrival; do so if plans change
- **Cancellation fees**: If you cancel after deadline, hotel charges fee; you are liable for fee (not reimbursed)
- **Early checkout**: No refund (policy of most hotels)
- **Extending stay**: Contact hotel directly; standard rate applies (corporate rate may not extend automatically)

## 5. Ground Transportation Booking

### 5.1 Rental Cars

**Vehicle class**: Economy or mid-size (e.g., Toyota Corolla, Honda Civic, Ford Focus)
- Upgrade to premium/luxury is employee cost
- SUV or truck upgrade must be justified (e.g., business in snow/off-road country)

**Rental company**: Hertz, Avis, Enterprise, Budget (all have corporate rates)
- Expensify shows available rental companies and rates
- Book via Expensify for corporate rate (10–20% discount)

**Insurance**: Company's commercial auto liability insurance covers rental cars; decline rental company's insurance (save $15–30/day)

**Fuel**: Return car with full tank; if you don't, rental company charges inflated fuel rate (~$2.50–3.00/gallon). Top up at local station before return.

### 5.2 Rideshare (Uber, Lyft)

- **Service level**: UberX or Lyft (base tier); avoid UberXL, Black, or Lyft Lux (premium)
- **Booking**: Use corporate account if available; Uber is integrated into Expensify
- **Tips**: 15–20% tip is reasonable and reimbursable
- **Expensing**: Submit Uber/Lyft receipt in Expensify within 30 days

### 5.3 Taxi and Public Transportation

- **Taxi**: Yellow cab, Lyft Pink (mid-tier), local car service acceptable
- **Parking validation**: Request hotel/venue to validate parking; company covers validated rate
- **Valet parking**: Not reimbursed (use self-parking or rideshare instead)
- **Public transit**: Bus, train, subway; receipt/card statement sufficient

### 5.4 Personal Vehicle Mileage

If you drive your personal vehicle for business travel:
- **Reimbursement rate**: IRS standard mileage rate ($0.655/mile as of 2026)
- **Minimum distance**: Threshold typically 100 miles (under 100 miles, often not worth mileage claim vs. rental car)
- **Calculation**: Round-trip distance × rate (e.g., 200 miles × $0.655 = $131 reimbursement)
- **Documentation**: Submit Expensify mileage log with start/end locations, dates, and business purpose

**Cost comparison**: For trips >150 miles, rental car is usually cheaper or equivalent to mileage reimbursement.

## 6. Travel with Sensitive Data

When traveling with corporate devices containing confidential or restricted data:
- **Encryption required**: Device must be encrypted (BitLocker, FileVault) per **Data Classification & Retention Policy**
- **VPN required**: Access corporate systems only via VPN from hotel/external networks
- **MFA required**: Multi-factor authentication (TOTP, hardware key) mandatory for VPN access
- **Lost device**: Immediately notify IT and security@northwind.com; may trigger incident response

See **Expense & Travel Policy** section 5 (Travel with Sensitive Data) for full data security requirements.

## 7. Travel Reimbursement and Expense Submission

### 7.1 Submission Deadline
- **Within 30 days** of trip completion (preferred)
- **Absolute deadline**: 90 days; expenses submitted after 90 days are not reimbursed

### 7.2 Required Documentation
- **Flights**: E-ticket (auto-attached via Expensify)
- **Hotels**: Confirmation email and final folio (itemized bill at checkout)
- **Rental car**: Rental agreement (auto-attached if booked via Expensify)
- **Meals**: Receipt for all meals >$75 (itemized); <$75 optional (estimated OK)
- **Ground transportation**: Uber/Lyft email receipt, taxi receipt, or mileage log

### 7.3 Submission Process
1. **Open Expensify expense report** created from travel request
2. **Attach receipts**: Photo via mobile app or PDF upload
3. **Categorize expenses**: Travel, meals, entertainment, etc.
4. **Add notes**: Business purpose, attendees, key outcomes (if applicable)
5. **Submit to manager**: Manager reviews and approves within 3 business days
6. **Finance review and payment**: Within 2–3 pay periods after manager approval

## 8. Exceptions and Appeals

### 8.1 Over-Budget Travel
If actual travel cost exceeds estimated approval:
- **Variance <10%**: Automatic reimbursement (market price fluctuation expected)
- **Variance 10–20%**: Finance reviews; reimbursed if justified (e.g., last-minute booking due to flight cancellation)
- **Variance >20%**: Escalated to VP; may require retroactive approval or partial reimbursement

### 8.2 Emergency Travel
If business requires urgent travel (no 14-day advance booking):
1. **Notify manager and VP immediately** (phone call, not email)
2. **Get verbal approval** for emergency travel
3. **Book travel** at lowest available fare (premium class OK if only option)
4. **Submit exception form** with business justification and manager signature
5. **Finance approves retroactively** (rare; must show genuine business urgency)

Example: Key customer escalation requires immediate attendance; sales VP approves emergency travel.
