# Pricing & Discount Policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes Northwind Cloud's list pricing, discount authority, and margin guardrails. All sales team members and Deal Desk must comply to maintain profitability and consistent positioning.

## 2. List Pricing (Effective 2026-01-01)

| Tier | Monthly Commitment | Annual Commitment | Data Volume Cap | Support Tier | Primary Use Case |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | $500 | $5,400 | 1 TB/mo | Email support | SMB, pilot projects |
| **Professional** | $1,500 | $16,200 | 10 TB/mo | Support SLA & Tiers Level 2 | Mid-market standard |
| **Enterprise** | $5,000 | $54,000 | Unlimited | Support SLA & Tiers Level 1 | Large-scale, multi-cloud |

## 3. Discount Eligibility

### 3.1 Automatic Discounts (No Approval Required)
- **Annual Commitment:** 10% off monthly rate if customer commits to 12-month contract.
- **Multi-Year (3+ years):** Additional 5% off annual rate.
- **Nonprofits & educational institutions:** 25% off standard pricing (requires 501(c)(3) documentation).

### 3.2 Discretionary Discounts (Approval Required)

| Scenario | Discount Range | Approval Authority | Justification Required |
|---|---|---|---|
| Competitive pressure (proven quote) | 5–15% | Sales VP | Competitor name, quote amount |
| Expansion of existing customer | 5–10% | Deal Desk | Forecast revenue increase |
| Multi-product bundle (add professional services) | 5–12% | CFO | Total contract value, margin impact |
| Strategic account / logo acquisition | 10–25% | CFO + VP Sales | Strategic rationale (e.g., foothold in vertical) |

### 3.3 Prohibited Discounts
- **Discounts >25%** without CEO approval (rare exception; requires Board-level escalation).
- **Discounts based solely on "customer budget constraints"** without competitive or strategic justification.
- **One-time discounts below cost** (gross margin <50% on any contract).

## 4. Professional Services Pricing

Professional services (implementation, custom integration, training) are sold separately and not subject to SaaS discounts. Standard rates:
- **Scoping & design:** $200/hr (billed hourly).
- **Implementation & integration:** $250/hr.
- **Training & enablement:** $150/hr.
- **Custom development:** $300/hr (requires VP Engineering approval).

Bundled discounts on professional services (e.g., 10% off for 50+ hours) require VP Engineering approval.

## 5. Margin & Profitability Guardrails

1. **Minimum gross margin:** 70% on SaaS recurring revenue. Contracts with <65% margin require CFO escalation.
2. **Payback period:** Average customer payback period (ACV / monthly churn) must exceed 24 months.
3. **CAC ratio:** Customer acquisition cost (total sales & marketing spend / new customers acquired) must maintain LTV:CAC ratio >3:1.

## 6. Volume & Expansion Pricing

- **Volume discounts:** If a customer adds 5+ seats or increases data volume by 50%, offer 5–8% expansion discount (approved via Deal Desk).
- **Multiseat pricing:** Negotiable only for >20 seats; base calculation: Tier price × (0.85 × number of seats).
- **Seat overages:** Any usage >contracted seats billed at 1.5× per-seat rate at month-end.

## 7. Promotional Discounts

- **New Year / product launch promo:** Up to 20% off for 3-month trial period, approved by CMO.
- **Seasonal promotions:** December year-end campaigns approved by CMO & CFO.
- **Beta program discounts:** Beta customers get 30–40% discount first 6 months (transition to standard pricing thereafter).

## 8. Pricing Review Cycle

- **Quarterly:** Finance & Sales leadership review discount utilization, margin trending, and churn by discount cohort.
- **Annually (Q1):** List price adjustment based on cost inflation and market positioning; approved by CFO, communicated to Sales.

## 9. Violations & Enforcement

Sales team members who circumvent approval thresholds (e.g., quoting discounts without authorization) face:
1. First offense: Written warning + mandatory Deal Desk training.
2. Second offense: Compensation clawback on affected deals + formal disciplinary review.
3. Egregious violations: Termination; contract may be renegotiated or voided if signed without authority.

## 10. Integration with RMA & Refund Policy

- If a customer requests refund within 30 days due to unsuitability, the discount applied to original contract remains in force.
- Refunds >$10K require CFO approval (see RMA & Refund Policy, section 4).
