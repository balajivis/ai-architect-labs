# Expense & Travel Policy

## 1. Travel Authorization and Budget

All travel must be approved by your manager and VP before booking. Exceptions exist for pre-approved recurring travel (e.g., monthly office visits for remote employees).

### 1.1 Travel Approval Workflow

1. **Submit travel request** in Expensify at least 14 days before travel date
   - Include: destination, travel dates, purpose (conference, customer meeting, business development)
   - Estimated total cost (flight + hotel + meals + ground transportation)

2. **Manager approval** within 3 business days

3. **VP approval** if total estimated cost > $2,000

4. **Finance approval** (automatic if under $2,500; manual review if > $2,500)

5. **Book flights/hotels** only after approval is received

### 1.2 Travel Budget Tiers

| Tier | Annual Budget | Per-Trip Max | Examples |
|------|---------------|---|---|
| **Individual Contributor** | $5,000 | $2,500 | Local conferences, customer meetings |
| **Manager** | $8,000 | $4,000 | Customer site visits, regional conferences |
| **Director/VP** | $15,000 | $7,500 | Cross-country travel, trade shows, board meetings |
| **Executive** | Unlimited | $15,000 per trip | Unlimited annual; VP approval required over $15K/trip |

**Annual budget resets** January 1; unused budget does not roll over.

## 2. Flights and Ground Transportation

### 2.1 Flights
- **Booking class**: Domestic flights must be economy class
- **Exceptions**: Flights over 6 hours may be business class if approved by VP (and VP+Finance if cost > $3,000)
- **Booking window**: Book at least 14 days in advance to ensure lowest fares
- **Airlines**: Preferred carrier is United (corporate rate available); any US carrier acceptable
- **Baggage**: Company covers standard baggage fees (1 checked bag); extra baggage is employee responsibility

### 2.2 Ground Transportation
- **Rentals**: Economy or mid-size rental cars only; upgrade at employee cost
- **Rideshare**: Uber/Lyft acceptable; use UberX or similar base tier (not UberX, UberXL)
- **Taxi/rideshare tips**: 15–20% is reasonable
- **Parking**: Standard rates covered; valet parking not reimbursed

### 2.3 Personal Vehicles
If you use your personal vehicle for business travel, mileage is reimbursed at the current IRS standard rate (typically $0.655 per mile). Rental car is preferred if total mileage exceeds 200 miles.

## 3. Lodging

### 3.1 Hotel Standards

| City Type | Max Nightly Rate (Before Taxes) |
|-----------|---|
| Major metro (NYC, SF, LA) | $250 |
| Mid-size city | $150 |
| Small town | $100 |

Rates are pre-tax. Taxes and mandatory fees are fully reimbursed.

### 3.2 Hotel Booking
- Use Northwind's corporate rate agreements (check Expensify for negotiated rates)
- Book hotels in advance to secure lower rates
- If hotel is full, next-best hotel at comparable price is acceptable
- Upgrade to suite or premium room is employee cost

### 3.3 Hotel Incidentals
- Meals at hotel restaurant: Reimbursable as meal expense (see Meals section)
- Wi-Fi: Reimbursable if not included
- Laundry/dry cleaning: Reimbursable for trips longer than 5 days
- Pay-per-view movies, minibar: NOT reimbursable
- Phone charges, resort fees (if non-negotiable): Reimbursable if documented

## 4. Meals

### 4.1 Meal Allowances

Employees may expense meals while traveling. Per diem limits (employee's total meal expense for day):

| Meal Type | Max Cost |
|-----------|---|
| Breakfast | $15 |
| Lunch | $25 |
| Dinner | $50 |
| **Daily total** | **$85** |

**Receipts required for all meals over $75.** Meals under $75 may be claimed without receipt (estimated cost is acceptable).

### 4.2 Meal Guidelines
- **Solo meals**: Expense your meal
- **Team meals**: One person from the group may expense a meal for the team if team is on approved business travel
- **Client meals**: Allowed; client's meal may be expensed. Include description of attendees and business purpose on receipt
- **Alcohol**: Reimbursable if part of a meal with clients or team; personal minibar use is not reimbursed

### 4.3 Meal Expense Documentation

When submitting meal expense:
1. Attach receipt (photo via Expensify camera app is acceptable)
2. If receipt does not show itemization, add note describing what you purchased
3. If attendees were present, list their names

## 5. Travel with Sensitive Data

When traveling with corporate data, follow **Data Classification & Retention Policy** rules:

- **Confidential data**: Device must be encrypted (BitLocker, FileVault)
- **Restricted data**: Device must be encrypted + require MFA
- **Both**: You must use VPN when accessing corporate systems from hotel or external networks (see **Remote Access / VPN Guide**)

If you lose a device containing customer data while traveling:
1. Immediately notify security@northwind.com and it-support@northwind.com
2. Escalate per **Incident Response Runbook** (likely Sev-2 or Sev-3)
3. Provide details: device type, last known location, data classification

Remote work laptops are covered by company insurance; theft is reimbursable.

## 6. Airbnb and Alternative Lodging

Airbnb and other alternative lodging are acceptable if:
- Nightly rate does not exceed hotel limits (see Lodging Standards section)
- Property has at least 4.8-star rating
- Approval is obtained from manager before booking

## 7. Conference Attendance

Conferences are approved business travel if:
- Conference directly relates to your role (e.g., engineering conference for engineers)
- Manager and VP approve attendance
- Conference cost is within annual travel budget

### 7.1 Conference Costs Covered
- Registration/admission fee
- Flight and ground transportation
- Hotel (per nightly limit)
- Meals (per diem)

### 7.2 Conference Costs NOT Covered
- Companion travel (spouse, friend, family member)
- Entertainment or recreational activities beyond conference hours
- Extended stay beyond conference dates (extra vacation days)

## 8. Expense Submission and Reimbursement

### 8.1 Submission Deadline
- Submit all expenses in Expensify **within 30 days** of trip completion
- Expenses submitted after 90 days are not reimbursed
- Receipts must be attached (photos, PDFs, or email confirmations)

### 8.2 Reimbursement Timeline
- Finance team processes submitted expenses within 5 business days
- Reimbursement is processed via direct deposit within 2–3 pay periods

### 8.3 Receipt Requirements

**Receipts are required for:**
- Any expense over $25 (except meals under $75)
- All flights and hotels
- All ground transportation over $25
- All meals over $75
- All entertainment or special purposes

**Acceptable receipt formats:**
- Original paper receipt
- Email confirmation from vendor (e.g., Uber confirmation email)
- Photo of receipt (via Expensify mobile app)
- Credit card statement extract (if itemized)

**Receipts are NOT required for:**
- Meals under $75 (but description is helpful)
- Tips under $10 (but must be itemized on receipt)

## 9. Violations and Enforcement

- **First offense**: Verbal warning; expense reimbursed if reasonable
- **Second offense**: Expense not reimbursed; mandatory expense compliance training
- **Third offense**: Suspension of travel authorization for 6 months

Examples of violations:
- Booking premium cabin without VP approval
- Staying at hotel over nightly limit
- Claiming personal entertainment as business expense
- Falsifying receipts or descriptions

## 10. Corporate Credit Card

Northwind provides corporate credit cards to frequent travelers and managers. Corporate card use is governed by the same expense policy. Monthly card statements are audited by Finance.
