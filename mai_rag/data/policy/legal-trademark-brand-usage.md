# Trademark & Brand Usage Policy

## Purpose
This policy governs the correct use, protection, and registration of Northwind Technologies' trademarks, logos, and brand assets. Consistent brand usage strengthens Northwind's market position and legal protections.

## 2. Trademark Use Guidelines

**Proper Usage**:
- Always use the ® symbol after **Northwind Cloud** and **Northwind** when used as product names (e.g., "Northwind Cloud®")
- Use TM symbol if mark is not yet registered (e.g., "Northwind DataBridge™")
- Use the registered logo, not knockoffs or restyled versions
- Maintain consistent capitalization and spacing (no "northwind" in lowercase in marketing materials)
- Use the mark as an adjective, not a verb (correct: "use Northwind Cloud"; incorrect: "to Northwind your data")

**Prohibited Usage**:
- Do not modify, distort, or animate the logo without Legal approval
- Do not use Northwind trademarks in generic ways (e.g., "this northwind solution" to refer to competitors' products)
- Do not register company or product names that confuse Northwind trademarks with unrelated services
- Do not grant third parties the right to use Northwind marks except under written agreements approved by General Counsel

## 3. Third-Party Use of Northwind Marks

**Customers** may use Northwind trademarks in:
- Case studies (pre-approved by Marketing)
- "Powered by Northwind Cloud" references with approved logos
- Internal documentation and presentations

**Partners & Resellers** may use Northwind marks only under a reseller agreement approved by Legal, which includes:
- Scope of permitted use (e.g., "Northwind Cloud Partner" only)
- Required attribution and disclaimers
- Restrictions on modification
- Termination clause if relationship ends

**Competitors & Unaffiliated Parties** may not use Northwind marks without written permission. Any unauthorized use is reported to General Counsel for cease-and-desist action.

## 4. Logo & Design Asset Management

**Official Logos** — All approved logos, icons, and brand guidelines are maintained in Northwind's Brand Portal (internal Sharepoint).

**Usage Approval**:
- Internal communications: Marketing team approval sufficient
- External marketing (press, trade shows, ads): Marketing + General Counsel approval
- Customer-facing product UI: VP Product + General Counsel approval
- Licensing to third parties (merch, co-marketing): General Counsel approval required

**Maintenance**:
- Primary logo (color) for use on light backgrounds
- Secondary logo (white) for use on dark backgrounds
- Do not recolor, rotate, or distort logos
- Minimum clear space around logo: 0.5 inches from other graphics
- Minimum size: 1 inch wide for print, 100px wide for digital

## 5. Domain Names & Social Media

**Domain Registration**:
- All domains containing "northwind", "northwindcloud", or other key marks are registered and renewed annually
- Any new domain using a similar mark requires Legal clearance before purchase
- Domains are registered in Northwind's name through an approved registrar (currently GoDaddy and DNSMADEEASY)

**Social Media Handles**:
- All official Northwind social media accounts (LinkedIn, Twitter, GitHub, etc.) use the official `@northwindtech` or `@northwindcloud` handle
- Marketing maintains a list of official accounts
- Employees may use personal social media but should not impersonate official Northwind accounts

## 6. Trademark Registration & Renewal

**Current Registrations**:
- **USPTO**: Northwind Technologies (word), Northwind Cloud (word + design), logos
- **EU**: Northwind Technologies, Northwind Cloud, associated designs
- **WIPO**: International registrations in Madrid System

**Renewal Schedule**:
- All U.S. trademarks renew every 10 years; Northwind's renewals are staggered (2024, 2025, 2026 schedule maintained by General Counsel)
- Affidavits of use (Section 8) are filed every 10 years to maintain registration
- General Counsel monitors renewal dates and ensures timely filing

**New Mark Registration**:
- Before investing in a new product name or logo, General Counsel conducts a trademark search to ensure no conflicts
- Approval from CEO + VP Marketing before filing application
- Applications are filed in the U.S., EU, and key international markets (Japan, Australia, Canada) to protect market position

## 7. Policing & Enforcement

**Monitoring for Infringement**:
- General Counsel monitors USPTO filings, domain registrations, and social media for similar marks that could create confusion
- Third-party use of Northwind marks without permission is reported to General Counsel
- Suspected counterfeit or infringing products are escalated to CEO + General Counsel

**Cease-and-Desist**: If third-party unauthorized use is identified, Northwind sends a cease-and-desist letter demanding:
- Cessation of the infringing use within 10 days
- Destruction of infringing materials
- Confirmation of compliance in writing

**Litigation**: Material or willful infringement (counterfeits, direct trademark copying) may result in federal court litigation for injunctive relief and damages.

## 8. Employee & Contractor Attribution

- Employees may identify themselves as "working at Northwind Technologies" in professional bios and LinkedIn profiles
- Contractors should not claim to be "Northwind employees" but may reference contract work with Northwind (if disclosed in contract)
- Any employee-created content using Northwind trademarks should include proper attribution and link to official Northwind content
